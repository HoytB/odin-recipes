Instant Pot Rice

Rice that will change your life!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Directions

  1. Rinse rice until water runs clear
  2. Heat oil in Instant Pot
  3. Toast rice for about a minute
  4. Add water and salt
  5. Cook at high pressure for 6 mins, slow release for 10
  6. Fluff
  7. Enjoy!
