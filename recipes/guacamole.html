Guacamole

Guacamole is an amazing appitizer that everyone will love! Made with ripe Califoria avacados and perfectly seasoned it will be a hit!

Ingredients

  • Avacados
  • Lemon juice
  • Cilantro
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Place Avacados, Lemon juice, Cilantro, and Salt into a bowl.
  2. Mix well!
  3. Enjoy!
